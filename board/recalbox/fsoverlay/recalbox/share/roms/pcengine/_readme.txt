## RECALBOX - SYSTEM PCENGINE##

Put your pcengine roms in this directory.

Rom files must have a ".pce" extension.

You can download pcengine roms on planetemu :
http://www.planetemu.net/roms/nec-pc-engine

You can download pcengine cd roms on mondemul :
http://www.mondemul.net/zisos.php?genre=liste&type=pcecd
